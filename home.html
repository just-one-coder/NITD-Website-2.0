FAQS

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the login and password for the portal?

There is an option "Forgot Password'' on the login page. On entering your id you will get a password generation link on your NIT Delhi webmail. This needs to be done.

What documents do we need to upload for the verification for off-campus interns and how?

If your internship is ongoing then upload the offer letter for your ongoing internship and in case your internship is over upload your internship completion certificate mentioning the date of completion.

What are all those sections that will be on second page and can we write anything on that page?

The second page will have your approved PORs, ECAs, Courses Done and JEE Rank. The former two are taken from the E-certification portal and the latter two are provided by the UG Section. You have the liberty to show the entries on the second page using toggle switches.

Can we apply off-campus to a company which comes to NITD but not for our department?

No, you can not apply to companies that visit on-campus even if they aren't open for your department. You can raise requests through the nucleus team of your Department to make your department eligible when the company registers.

Can we add the ongoing courses/projects (on-campus) on the verified page?

The verified page(second page) contains points approved by Ecertification or by UG Section. Students do not have the liberty to add points on their own on the verified page. Only completed courses can be mentioned on this page.

How will we know that we are shortlisted for a certain company's interview?

In the Applications section, your status will be updated to shortlisted. We may also put lists of company shortlists in the notifications section of the portal.

Where can we see the list of companies that are coming to the campus?

In the 'Companies on Campus' section, all the companies visiting the campus will be visible.

Where will the test links be shared and on which platform would they be conducted?

The test links will either be shared on the OCS portal or mailed to you. The testing platform will depend on the company.
